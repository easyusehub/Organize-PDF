# 📂 Organize PDF | EasyUseHub

**Organize PDF** is a free online tool to reorder, select, and manage pages of your PDF files directly in your browser. No API, no uploads — your files remain private and secure.

## ✨ Features
- ✅ Reorder PDF pages via drag & drop  
- ✅ Click to select/deselect pages  
- ✅ Live page preview with thumbnails  
- ✅ Export only selected pages or all pages  
- ✅ Fully client-side (no API required)  
- ✅ Max file size: 30MB  
- ✅ Premium UI & mobile-friendly design  
- ✅ Ad placeholders & All PDF Tools / Home buttons  

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Upload your PDF file (Max 30MB)  
2. All pages appear as live thumbnails  
3. Click pages to select/deselect (highlighted when selected)  
4. Drag & drop pages to reorder  
5. Click **Save PDF** to generate your new file  
6. Download your organized PDF instantly  

> 🔒 All processing happens in your browser — your PDF never leaves your device.
